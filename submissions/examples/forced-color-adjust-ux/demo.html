Color Adjust Utilities

forced-color-adjust + print-color-adjust

What this demonstrates:
.fca-preserve applies forced-color-adjust: none and print-color-adjust: exact to preserve custom background colors in Windows High Contrast Mode and when printing. Without these, the browser strips background colors in both scenarios.
