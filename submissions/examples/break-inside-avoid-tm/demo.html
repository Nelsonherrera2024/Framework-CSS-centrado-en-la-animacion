break-inside

Preventing Unwanted Breaks in Columns

The break-inside property controls how elements behave across column, page, and region breaks. Essential for multi-column layouts where cards should not be split.

Card Grid: break-inside Comparison
Notice how the "avoid" cards stay intact across columns while the "auto" cards may be split. Resize the browser to see the effect more clearly.
