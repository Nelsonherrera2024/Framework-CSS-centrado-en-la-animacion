Chip States Visual Bug

Chip hover, active, selected, and disabled states are visually indistinguishable.

Issue #5874

The Problem

Chips lack distinct visual styles for :hover, :active, [aria-selected="true"], and [disabled] states. Users cannot tell if a chip is interactive, currently selected, or disabled.
