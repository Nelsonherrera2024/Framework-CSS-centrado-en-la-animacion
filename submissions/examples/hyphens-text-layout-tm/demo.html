Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. The arrangement of type involves selecting typefaces, point sizes, line lengths, line-spacing (leading), and letter-spacing (tracking).

Good hyphenation improves reading comfort and saves horizontal space, especially in narrow columns like sidebars and footnotes. The CSS hyphens property gives developers fine-grained control without touching the source HTML.

In multilingual layouts supporting German or Arabic, automatic hyphenation is essential because these languages have long compound words that frequently require breaking at line ends. The browser dictionary handles the linguistic rules automatically.
