Scroll to Top

FAB-style button appears after 300px scroll with fade + slide animation

Scroll down to see the button appear in the bottom-right corner.

Click it to smoothly scroll back to the top.

Why a Scroll-to-Top Button?

On long pages, users often scroll down significantly and need a quick way to return to the top without manual scrolling or reaching for the keyboard. A fixed scroll-to-top button improves navigation efficiency and overall user experience.

Behavior

  • Hidden by default (offscreen or opacity 0)
  • Appears with a fade-in + slide-up animation once the page is scrolled past 300px
  • Fixed position in the bottom-right corner
  • Smooth scrolls to the top of the page on click
  • Hover effect for visual feedback
  • Respects prefers-reduced-motion

Usage

<button id="scroll-top-btn" onclick="scrollToTop()">
  &#8593;
</button>
