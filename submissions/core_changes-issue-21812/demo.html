FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to become fluent?+

Most learners reach conversational fluency in 3–6 months with 15 minutes of daily practice. Advanced fluency typically takes 6–12 months depending on the language and your commitment.

Can I learn multiple languages at once?+

Yes! Premium and Family plans allow you to learn up to 3 languages simultaneously. Our AI creates separate learning paths for each language and prevents vocabulary confusion.

Is the AI speaking practice accurate?+

Our speech recognition engine is trained on over 2 million hours of native speech across all 30+ languages. It provides phoneme-level feedback on your pronunciation.

Can I cancel my subscription?+

Absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period — no questions asked.
